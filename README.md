## About Me
I am 28 years old at the moment of writing this, trying to switch career. I have been working for more than 5 years in healthcare (Pflegefachkraft) in Germany and am now trying to transition into cybersecurity.
After finishing my IT High School and dropping out of University, I got a chance to move to Germany in search of a more prosperous life. I quickly did my B1 German language course and moved to Germany in 2019.
The next day I started my Pflegefachkraft Ausbildung (School). Why I chose this one is simple, It was the quickest way to move. My plan back then was to finish it and then pursue something with IT, but that didn't go as planned.

After finishing the Ausbildung, I stayed in the field for a few private reasons but one of them was that I became too comfortable and what I achieved was good enough. The longer I stayed in this field, the more I recognized that there is not much room to improve and basically, I am stuck here.
The years passed, the more frustrated I was because of it. I wanted to do something but I didn't know where to start. I always loved IT, but I had no idea what I could do.
And one day there was an AI drama. Everybody switched to Claude.ai, I hated AI myself, never had a really good use of it. But I was curious why everybody moved to Claude. I checked it out and, by sheer luck, I wrote to it that I was not satisfied with my situation and behold
He gave me the most motivational speech with the spreadsheets, roadmap, plan literally everything how to switch career. It told me about Cybersecurity and I was hooked. I researched everything about it, courses, certs, found job listings in Germany, and I had a plan.
I had a plan to learn it, to master it and finally get out of my comfort zone.
I know it will be hard, stressful, but I will keep giving everything to succeed.
And that's the story of how I plan to switch career, if I ever manage it, but every step I will be documenting here.
